Welcome to Zombie Circles!
Use the arrow keys to move (the black circle) and avoid zombies (red circles) at all costs!
Collect green circles and stay alive to get more points!
Be careful! If you hold your arrow keys too long your circle will get tired and you'll be slower!
Zombies WILL chase you if you get too close.
Keep moving - staying in one spot will eventually cause the zombies to horde.
